Unlike traditional AI prompts that load everything upfront, gICM uses a **three-layer architecture**:

1. **Skills** (5-15KB) - Load only when triggered by keywords
2. **Commands** (2-8KB) - One-shot workflows for specific tasks

Your AI assistant only loads what it needs, when it needs it.
